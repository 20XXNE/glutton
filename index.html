WHO WE ARE

Joseph Kudrak, Owner/Chef

Joseph was born & raised in Derby, Connecticut where he found his love for the Culinary Arts early in life. He was intrigued early with cooking as his grandfather was a chef, and cooking Sunday brunch was a tradition for the family. Joseph took his first restaurant job at 14 washing dishes and never looked back. He began his Culinary Training at Emmett O'Brien Vocational School. After high school, Joseph attended Paul Smiths College in New York State. He completed his training and got his first taste of Las Vegas with an externship at the MGM Grand, he then returned to New York and graduated with an A.A.S. in Culinary Arts with a minor in Baking. After working in a couple restaurants in western New York, he couldn't stay away from Las Vegas and quickly made his way back to Sin City. Joseph honed his skills in areas such as production kitchens, buffets, and steakhouses, working in 4 diamond local casinos and restaurants. Joseph was recently invited to Iowa to cook for 400 farmers from Niman Ranch and personally thank them for their contributions to the food community. Joseph's passion for food lies in the simplicity of the flavors having a deep appreciation and respect for ingredients, where they are from, and the people who produce them. He plans to continue that commitment to the community of Las Vegas by bringing his style of cooking to the Downtown Community.
